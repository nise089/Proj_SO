The profit choice stage

In this stage, the owner chooses how the profit is used.
S/he can choose between three options:

  • Owner bonus: the profit is paid to the owner as a bonus
  • Worker bonus: the profit is equally distributed among all workers as a bonus, i.e., each worker receives 1/3 of the profit
  • Donation: the profit is donated to Doctors without borders

Note that the payoffs received from the profit choice are bonuses in addition to the payoffs from the working stage:
Independent of the owner's profit choice, the owner always receives the dividend, and the workers always receive the fixed wage plus their individual piece-rate.
