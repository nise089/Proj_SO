The selling choice stage

The owner selects a price P between 0 and 100 for which s/he is willing to sell the company.

Simultaneously, a computer randomly draws a price offer Z from the same range.

  • If the owner’s price is smaller or equal to the computer’s price (P < Z), the company is sold, and the experiment ends. The owner earns the offered price Z.
  • If the owner’s price is higher than the computer’s price (P > Z), the company is not sold, and the production continues with the next round.

Caution! The selling choice is only available starting from round 2. In round 1, the company cannot be sold.
