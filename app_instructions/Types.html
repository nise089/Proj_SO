The company types

There are four company types: A, B, C, and D.
You will be randomly assigned to one of them.
Company A was just explained. The other company types differ to company A in 1. the owner’s role and 2. the owner’s decision space.

  1. In companies C and D, the owner is a worker her/himself.
  2. In companies B and D, the profit cannot be used as an owner bonus, and if the company is sold, the price offer Z goes to ‘Doctors without borders’.
